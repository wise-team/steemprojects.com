Contributing to {{ SITE_TITLE }}

This website is an entirely volunteer driven effort. That includes coding time and hosting costs. If you wish to help or show your gratitude, there are plenty of ways in which you can do that:

  • Make sure that entries about your favorites projects are in the good shape, edit them if they don't
  • If your favorite project is not listed, you can help by adding it. Here is guideline which says how to do that properly.
  • Did you found a bug? If it is not listed on our github, please let us know about it!
  • Are you a developer? Great! We always keep a list of issues with a label good first issue. You can help us by resolving one of them :)
  • Do you own a Steem-related project? Add a signature to your post about a possibility of adding your project to user's favourite projects.
  • Add SteemProjects to your favorite projects :)
